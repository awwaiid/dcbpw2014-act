Venue - Silver Spring Civic Building at Veterans Plaza

NOTE: See VenueDetail on the wiki for day-of and to-the-minute detail of meeting rooms, wifi info, etc.

The Silver Spring Civic Building (SSCB) is located in Downtown Silver Spring, walkable from a DC metro stop. It is located near food and other attractions! SSCB is in a redeveloped old downtown area, still known as Downtown Silver Spring (with hip internet hashtag #DTSS). The surrounds a central street that becomes pedestrian-only on weekends.

Silver Spring Civic Building
One Veteran's Place
Silver Spring, MD 20910

 

Public Transit / Parking

The SSCB is about five blocks from the Silver Spring Metro station on the Red Line. There are feeders from various airports and Amtrak trains into the metro rail system. It is also accessible on the 70/79 and S2/S4 bus lines from DC, plus a number of Ride-On buses from Montgomery County. Info on busses here.

The county runs municipal parking garages that are free on evenings, weekends, and holidays. The nearest parking garage is called the "Town Hall" garage, and is across the street from the SSCB. If the Town Hall garage is full, the Wayne Avenue and Spring-Colesville garages are within a couple minutes' walk. Here is a list of local parking.
